1. Deekshabhoomi – A Holy Place

One of the best Nagpur destinations renowned as Dhamma Chakra Stupa, this is a holy place that is located 5 km south-west from Nagpur City. Built by the famous architect Sheo Dan Mal, his stupa derives its inspiration from Sanchi Stupa. Established in memory of Dr. Ambedkar, this place consists of a bronze statue of Buddha along with a sacred tree positioned at the centre. Signified as the most serene place, Deeksha Bhoomi is likely to be on the top of the best places to visit in Nagpur list.

Location: Ambazari Road Timings: Open all-day

2. Shri Swaminarayan Mandir – Offer Your Prayers

One of the finest tourist places in Nagpur, the temple is an important landmark in the Vidarbha region. Offer your prayers and indulge yourself in spiritual activities that calm your soul. You can find the idols of many prominent gods including Shri Hanumanji, Shri Shrinathji, and Shri Lakshmi-Narayan Dev. VIsit for some different experiences in the city of Nagpur.

Location: Swaminarayan Marg, Middle Ring road, Nagpur, Maharashtra 440 030 Timings: 8:00 am – 12:00 pm; 4:00 pm – 8:30 pm

3. Ramtek – Seek Blessings

Ramtek is a beautiful city situated in Nagpur district. This city is an eminent religious place for all Hindu pilgrims. It is believed that when Lord Ram was in exile he stayed in Ramtek. You can visit the famous Ram Temple established by a Maratha ruler. Raghuji Bhonsale won a war and ordered the construction of this temple in the 18th century. This is also known for the Jain Temple that has statues of Jain Tirthankara. Do visit both the temples on your vacation to seek blessings.

Location: Nagpur Timings: Throughout the day

4.Koradi Temple – Dedicated To Goddess Durga

Koradi Temple in Nagpur is located on the Chhindwara Road. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga and remains busiest during Dussehra and Navratri celebrations. It is one of the Shakti Peeth and as the temple is located beside Koradi Lake, the place is often visited by tourists holidaying in the city.

Location: Shree Mahalaxmi Jagdamba Sansthan, Koradi, Kamptee, Maharashtra 441111 Timings: 6:00 AM to 12:30 PM & 4:00 PM to 9:00 PM
